About Nerine OS

Nerine OS is a Debian-based Linux distribution focused on gaming, customization, and a modern open-source experience.

Mission

Nerine OS aims to provide a high-performance Linux platform where users can game, create, and customize freely. Built with stability in mind, Nerine OS bridges modern technologies with an accessible experience.

Why Nerine?

Optimized for gaming and low-latency performance

Customized KDE, XFCE, and GNOME editions

Unified theming and branding across DEs

Strong Debian base with APT ecosystem

Open-Source Development

Nerine OS is entirely open-source. Contributions are welcome through GitHub. Community collaboration ensures security, quality, and continuous evolution.
